The Story

Solo Leveling follows Sung Jin-Woo, a hunter who starts as the weakest of them all, barely stronger than a normal human. After being left for dead in a dungeon, he awakens to a mysterious power that allows him to level up his abilities like in a video game.

As Jin-Woo grows stronger, he uncovers the secrets behind the dungeons appearing in the world and the true nature of his powers. The story combines intense action with deep character development as Jin-Woo transforms from the weakest hunter to one of the most powerful beings in existence.
